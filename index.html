What it does

  • Cross-platform CLI (Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Detects: emails, phone numbers, IPs, credit cards (Luhn), JWTs, AWS keys, UUIDs, IBAN, dates
  • Redaction styles: ***, [REDACTED:TYPE], or hash
  • Works with .txt, .log, .json, .csv
  • Runs 100% offline — nothing leaves your machine

Quickstart

# redact a file
ghostredact --in server.log --out clean.log

# or from stdin
cat dump.txt | ghostredact --style stars > clean.txt
